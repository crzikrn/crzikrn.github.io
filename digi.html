Digital Analog Representations of Us
ITP, NYU
New York, NY
2013

DIGITAL ANALOG REPRESENTATIONS OF US

The juxtaposition of analog and digital expressions on a digital canvas. Usage of facedetection and pixel brightness to simulate paintings in the style of expressionism. A digital tool to bring people together and have fun in front of the screen creating new expressive digital paintings. The more the merrier; the more people we have, more complete the digital canvas. It's about bringing out the expressive needs of humans and taking a memorable painting

Tools used: openCV, processing
