ABOUT

I recently graduated as a master's candidate at ITP, NYU. My passions are closely tied with computational media effects on society, education and religion. I love to translate perspectives to the users language; to move them and lit their dormant passions. I consider myself a digital media designer; detail-oriented visual designer and storyteller of raw human interactions. I am passionate to experiment with interactive platforms to communicate perspectives and culture.

On the side, I love programming languages and vector designs, and especially love to teach students. I have recently taught at Mongolia at Huree University in the Multimedia department. And am looking forward for more teaching opportunities in the future.
